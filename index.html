Crystal Collector

Here are the rules:


You are given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four crystals below. Clicking on a crystal adds a specific amount of points to your score.


In order to win the game, you must match your total score to the random number. You will lose the game if your score goes above the random number.


The value of each crystal is hidden until clicked. Each time the game starts, the value of each crystal also changes.
